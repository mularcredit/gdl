GDS Intake — July 2026 & New Enrolments Open

GDS Learning Centre Ltd is now accepting enrolments for the July 2026 intake. Whether you are starting your travel career or upgrading your existing GDS skills, this is your opportunity to train with Kenya’s premier GDS institute.

Our hands-on courses cover Amadeus and Galileo (Travelport) GDS platforms — flight booking, fare construction, ticketing, reservations management, and real-world travel operations. All sessions are delivered by certified industry professionals in Nairobi CBD.

What you’ll gain

  • Hands-on GDS platform training — Amadeus & Galileo (Travelport).
  • Flight booking, ticketing, fare construction, and reservations management.
  • Real-world travel industry scenarios led by certified instructors.
  • GDS Learning Centre certificate of completion upon graduation.
  • Career guidance and industry networking support.

Spaces are limited per intake. Contact us via WhatsApp or the enrolment form to secure your place for July 2026.
