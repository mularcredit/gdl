GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

A Global Distribution System (GDS) is the technology backbone of the travel industry — used by airlines, hotels, and travel agents worldwide to book flights, accommodation, and services in real time. Proficiency in GDS platforms like Amadeus and Galileo (Travelport) is a requirement for most travel and airline careers.

No prior experience is required. Our beginner courses are designed to take you from zero to job-ready. We also offer intermediate and expert-level programs for professionals looking to upgrade their skills.

Course duration varies by program. Short courses run for 2 weeks, standard programs are 4 weeks, and our expert masterclass runs 6 weeks. All courses are hands-on and practical, ensuring you leave ready for the workplace.

We offer certified training on three of the world's leading GDS platforms — Amadeus and Galileo (Travelport). You can choose to specialise in one or enrol in our combined program covering both systems.

Yes. Upon successful completion of your course, you will receive a GDS Learning Centre certificate of completion. Our training is aligned with industry standards, and we guide graduates on obtaining official GDS platform certifications.

You can enroll by contacting us via WhatsApp, phone, or our online form. Course fees start from KES 7,000 for short courses and from KES 14,000 for standard programs. Payment plans are available — reach out to discuss what works for you.
