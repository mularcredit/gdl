Course Overview

GDS Learning Centre Ltd is the premier institute for industry-driven GDS training. We are committed to equipping learners with the practical skills, confidence, and expertise needed to excel in the global travel industry.

Our hands-on courses cover Amadeus and Travelport GDS, flight booking, fare construction, ticketing, reservations, and travel operations. Learn from experienced instructors using real-world scenarios that prepare you for a successful travel career.

What you’ll Learn?

  • Encode & Decode airports, airlines, and city codes
  • Perform availability searches and fare shopping
  • Create, retrieve, and cancel air bookings
  • Issue, reissue, and process ticket refunds and revalidations
  • Handle SSR's, queues, fare building, and EMD's

At GDS Learning Centre Ltd we deliver practical, industry-driven GDS training that equips learners with the skills and confidence to excel in the global travel industry.
